    SSSDay Q&A

    November 01, 2021
    Conference: SSSDay 2021, Musée de Zoologie
    Location: Lausanne, CH
    Date and Time:
    Description: Q&A re the Swiss-universities funded eBiodiv and the EU Horizon 2020 funded BiCIKL

    Plazi will participate in the SSSDay organised by the Swiss Systematics Society (SSS) to answer questions regarding the Swiss universities funded eBiodiv project and the EU Horizon 2020 funded BiCIKL project. The event is hosted by the Musée de Zoologie Lausanne.
